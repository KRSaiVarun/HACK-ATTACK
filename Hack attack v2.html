// 01 — education

HOW ATTACKERS EXPLOIT YOU

Most hacks rely on human mistakes — weak passwords, curiosity clicks, and misplaced trust. Here's what you're up against.

🔑
Weak Passwords
Over 80% of breaches involve compromised credentials. "123456" gets cracked in milliseconds using brute-force tools.
🛡 Use 12+ character passwords with symbols.
🎣
Phishing Emails
Fake emails disguised as banks or Netflix. One click on a link and your credentials are silently stolen.
🛡 Always verify the sender's real domain.
📶
Public Wi-Fi
Attackers can "man in the middle" your connection — intercepting passwords and messages in real time.
🛡 Use a VPN on public networks.
👤
Social Engineering
Attackers psychologically manipulate you into revealing passwords — pretending to be IT support or a friend.
🛡 Never share credentials with anyone verbally.
